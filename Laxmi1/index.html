Smart Features of the System

QR-Based Reporting

Scan QR codes placed in classrooms, laboratories, hostels and campus facilities to instantly raise complaints.

Instant Notification

Complaints are automatically forwarded to the concerned HOD, Dean or Principal based on priority.

Real-Time Monitoring

Monitor complaints, resolutions and department-wise statistics in real time.

Secure Tracking

Students can track complaint status using a unique ticket ID generated automatically.

How It Works

Scan QR

Scan the QR code available in the classroom, lab or campus area.

Submit Report

Fill complaint details and submit the issue instantly.

Auto Assignment

Complaint automatically reaches HOD, Dean or Principal based on priority level.

Track Resolution

Monitor complaint progress from submission to completion using ticket ID.

System Workflow

Student

Raises complaint through QR-based reporting form.

Faculty

Receives assigned complaint after secure login.

Status Update

Faculty updates complaint status as Pending, In Progress or Resolved.

Tracking

Students track complaint resolution using Ticket ID.
